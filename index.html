About Us

MemberMinder is the SaaS for managing your climbing wall! We integrate a member management system, with an emphasis on safety, alongside with Point-of-Sale (POS) software. Using MemberMinder allows you to easily keep and track customisable data about your members, crucial to their well-being in the fastest and simplest of ways. We offer scheduling, online waivers, pre-booking and all the tools to avoid a bad situation.
